- Parameter changes:
	- in `setparamOrig´:
		- `std_shock_l_cpi´ setting removed
		- `std_shock_l_y´ set to 1 (irrelevant for the filtration, the shock size is determined by observed data)
	- in `setparamNew´: all the above, plus
		- `b1´ to 0.35 from 0.45, to increase forward-lookingness of the core Phillips curve
		- `c2´ to 1.2 from 0.2, to really be in line the the BNR calibration (they use a different definition of the nominal neutral rate, with inflation expectations)
		- `c3´ to 0.2 from 0.5, to be in line with the forecast model
		- `e1´/`e2´ to 0.5/0.5 from 0.2/0.2, to have a more reasonable UIP so that `l_z_gap´ responses are less persistent
